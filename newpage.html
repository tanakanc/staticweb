Brighter future is a non-profit charitable project, committed to improving the well-being of the underprivileged children at the ‘Home of Praise’ nursery in the ‘Klong Toey community. The nursery is the second home to children aged 6 months to 4 years who reside in this severely run-down neighborhood.
Why?
These children are presently striving for proper meals, health supplies and improved education. Do YOU want to live in a life where you only gain 5$ a day no matter how hard you try?
